About Pokhi

The skies of Assam are filled with incredible birdlife, yet information about these birds is often scattered across field guides, scientific publications, and online resources. Local Assamese names are particularly difficult to find, despite being an important part of the region's natural and cultural heritage.

To celebrate and preserve this heritage, Pokhi brings together bird photographs, Assamese names, conservation status, and basic identification information into a single accessible directory. By combining visual documentation with local nomenclature, the platform serves as a valuable resource for birdwatchers, photographers, students, educators, researchers, and anyone interested in Assam's rich avian diversity.

Whether you are identifying a bird, learning its Assamese name, or exploring Assam's remarkable avian diversity, Pokhi aims to make the birds of Assam easier to discover, understand, and appreciate. We invite you to explore Pokhi and celebrate the rich birdlife of Assam, one species at a time.
