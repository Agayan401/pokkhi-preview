Privacy Policy

Pokkhi respects your privacy. Information submitted through the "Suggest a Bird" form—including bird names, location, notes and contributor name—is used only to review submissions and improve the directory. We do not sell, rent or share personal information with third parties.

If analytics or similar services are introduced in the future, this policy will be updated accordingly.
